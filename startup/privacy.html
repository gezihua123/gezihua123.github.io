Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Celestial Oracle stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or share your personal information with any third party.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Celestial Oracle, we only collect the following information to provide our services:

  • Birth date and time — used to calculate your Bazi (Four Pillars) chart, fortune readings, and daily insights.
  • Gender — required for accurate Bazi calculations, as certain interpretations differ by gender.
  • Dream descriptions — text you enter for dream interpretation; processed entirely on-device.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All your data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage technologies:

  • MMKV — for settings, language preference, and privacy consent.
  • SQLite — for reading history, fortune rules, and interpretation data.

No data is ever transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers. Your information never leaves your device.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third party. We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or social media tracking that would transmit your data externally.

4. Data Deletion

You can delete all your stored data at any time:

  • Open Settings → Clear All Data

This action permanently removes all saved birth information, reading history, and preferences from your device. This action cannot be undone.

5. Remote Configuration

The app periodically downloads a lightweight configuration file from our public repository to adjust non-personal app behavior (such as feature availability). This configuration file contains no personal data and is cached locally for offline use.

6. Children's Privacy

Celestial Oracle is intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, there is no risk of children's data being exposed online.

7. Security

Since your data never leaves your device, the primary security consideration is physical access to your device. We recommend using your device's built-in security features (passcode, fingerprint, or face recognition) to protect your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
