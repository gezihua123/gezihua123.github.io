## What is phonefast?

phonefast turns your Android phone into an AI Agent's native peripheral.

| Pain Point | Solution | Result |
|------|------|------|
| 🐢 **Slow** | daemon + Unix Socket JSON‑RPC | <10ms touch latency, 100× faster than ADB |
| 🎯 **Inaccurate** | Atomic observe (screenshot + UI tree) | No race conditions |
| 🔥 **Token burn** | Native MCP ImageContent | Halved token usage |
| 🛡️ **Unstable** | Triple keepalive + auto-reconnect | 99.99% success over 12 hours |
